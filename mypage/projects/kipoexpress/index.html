Kipo Express

Website Design, Development

Kipo Express is a house moving and cleaning firm from Switzerland. They approached me with the need for a website for their services. Here's what I did for them.

After they've described their needs, I've came up with some concepts that would fit their needs and their customers needs. We decided that a one page website is the best way to present to their customers what they do and how they can get in touch with them as easy as possible.

My responsibility for this project was to design a website that is easy to use, shows the exact needed information, and after the design part, to turn it into a fully working website that will adapt on the screen size.
